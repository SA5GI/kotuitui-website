Stories from Riders

The Road to Feeling Unsafe

You've heard the stories on social media. The negative news, the feeling that public transport isn't what it used to be. It's easy to feel unsafe and disconnected from the people around you, especially on your daily commute. We believe your ride should be more than just a trip, it should be a reassuring and positive experience.

The Solution

Making Every Trip a Positive One

KŌTUITUI is a new way to connect with the people who drive our buses. We want you to see them not just as drivers, but as people who are part of our community.

Here's how it works...

Meet Your Driver

With our new app update, you can use the "Meet Your Driver" feature to leave a review and give a bus driver a badge for a great ride

Give Recognition

These badges are special because they can be worn by the bus drivers, showing their dedication and the positive impact they have

Share The Story

By sharing your positive experience, you help create a community where great service is celebrated, one trip at a time
